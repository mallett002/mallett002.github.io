BECOME A MEMBER

HOW IT WORKS

Sign up.

Learn More

There are 2 plans: weekly or monthly. Membership is $5 per week or month, and in as little as 3 weeks or months, you can ask for interest free money. The program works on generosity and reliablity.

Borrow.

Learn More

After 3 weeks or months, depending on which plan you choose, you can ask for $48, which you will pay back at $16 per week or month. When you have paid off a loan, you can move a level and ask for a higher amount.

Level up.

Learn More

As you pay your loans you move up in levels, which qualifies you to borrow more money. If you miss a payment, you cannot go to the next level. (You can catch up and try again.)

Reliable people should not have to pay high interest rates on their loans. We provide interest free withdrawals.

THE LEVELS

You pick a weekly or monthly plan. You have to complete one level before going to the next, that is, you have to keep up your weekly or monthly membership fee and your payback schedule to move to the next level of asking.
